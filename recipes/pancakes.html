Pancakes

Pancakes are simply the best, and pretty easy to make. Follow this recipe to make the BEST pancakes!

Pancakes (10 minute version)(Sam's Version)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Lemon juice
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine the flour, water, milk, eggs, lemon juice, and cinnamon.
  2. Turn stove on to medium high heat, melt butter in a skillet.
  3. Pour 1/2 a cup of pancake mixture onto pan. Flip after bubbles begin to appear in the surface.
  4. Remove from heat and repeat. Continue making pancakes until you have an aesthetically pleasing stack of pancakes on a plate.
  5. Serve with warmed up maple syrup and butter. Enjoy!
