Avocado toast is an absolute classic. This is one of my favorite dishes, because it's SO EASY to make!

Here's a simple recipe to get you started:

Sam's Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat toast
  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Get some bread
  2. Slap that bread in a toaster
  3. Grab an avocado
  4. Cut open that avocado
  5. chop that bad boy up
  6. Retrieve toast from toaster
  7. Slather on that avocado
  8. Sprinkle some salt and pep to finish it off
  9. Enjoy with some coffee and contemplation
