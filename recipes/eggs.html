Eggs over easy

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a skillet on medium high heat.
  2. Crack eggs and gently put in pan. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  3. Once the egg whites are opaque, gently flip the egg.
  4. Remove from heat once the eggs are cooked.
  5. Enjoy!
